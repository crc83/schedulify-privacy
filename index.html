description Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Serhii Belei ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Schedulify mobile application ("the App").

By using Schedulify, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled with care and transparency.

folder_open Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in via Google, we receive your name and email address from your Google account. This information is used to identify you and provide access to your personal account within the App.

Schedule & Absence Data

We store data you enter into the App, including your therapy schedules, absence records, therapist availability, and related organizational information. This data is essential for the App's core functionality.

Push Notification Token

If you enable push notifications, we collect a device token to deliver notifications to your device. This allows us to send you schedule updates, absence alerts, and other important information in real-time.

verified_user How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide access to your account
  • Display and sync your personal schedule and absence data
  • Send relevant push notifications such as schedule changes and reminders
  • Maintain and improve the App's functionality and user experience

Important: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Schedulify service.

security Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase infrastructure provided by Google LLC, hosted on Google Cloud servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

Our security measures include:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/SSL
  • Secure authentication via Google Sign-In
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and permission-based data access

schedule Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Schedulify service. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

When you request account deletion, we will process your request within 30 days and permanently delete all your personal information from our systems, except where we are required to retain certain data by law.

child_care Children's Privacy

Schedulify is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at the email address below and we will delete it promptly from our systems.
