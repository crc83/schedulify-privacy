Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Serhii Belei ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Schedulify mobile application ("the App").

By using Schedulify, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in via Google, we receive your name and email address from your Google account.

Schedule & Absence Data

We store data you enter into the App, including your class schedules, absence records, and related academic information.

Push Notification Token

If you enable push notifications, we collect a device token to deliver notifications to your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

· Authenticate your identity and provide access to your account

· Maintain and improve the App's functionality

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
